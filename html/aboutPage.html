ABOUT US

We are YadaYada: a team of junior developers with a strong passion for programming.

We love to familiarize ourselves with new technologies and undertake any task assigned to us with the utmost respect and enthusiasm!

We have worked on a variety of projects in the past, including reverse-engineered database structures, work with logic circuits, and the creation of an online calculator.
