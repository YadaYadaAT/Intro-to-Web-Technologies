Verdant Vigil

Surveying its realm from the high perch of a leaf, the green mantis stands as a true warrior of the plant world. This image invites us to look closer at the small, often overlooked defenders of the garden.

Did You Know? Mantises have a unique ear located on their belly that's especially tuned to the frequency of bat echolocation, their main predator.

Fascinating Fact: In a single bound, a mantis can leap over 40 times the length of its own body, making for an impressive display of agility.
