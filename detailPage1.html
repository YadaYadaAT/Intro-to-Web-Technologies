Green Embrace

A tender moment shared between man and mantis. This photo celebrates the gentle side of these misunderstood creatures, often seen as fierce predators.

Did You Know? Praying mantises are one of the few insects capable of turning their heads to look over their shoulders.

Fascinating Fact: Their vibrant green hue is not just for camouflage; it's also a deterrent to predators searching for a meal.
